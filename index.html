About Fiche

Fiche is a photography service company. Our goal is to provide customers with high-quality, stunning photoshoots at a competitive price. We operate in a B2B and B2C model, working with both individuals and businesses to provide personalised photography services.

Fiche is also incubated at Jansons Institute of Technology in Coimbatore, providing us with valuable resources and support, such as mentorship, access to funding, and networking opportunities.

Our team of experienced photographers, videographers, and editors work closely with each other and with customers to ensure that their needs are met and provide a personalised experience. We believe that every photo tells a story, and our goal is to capture the memories that matter most to our clients.

Booking and Payment Policy

1.Booking Process:

- Mobile App Booking: Users can book a photoshoot through the Fiche mobile app. The app allows users to select the type of photoshoot, date, time, and location. -Availability Confirmation: Once the booking request is submitted, users will receive a confirmation of availability.

2.Advance Payment:

- 50% Deposit: To secure a booking, users are required to pay 50% of the total photoshoot amount as an advance deposit. - Payment Methods: The advance payment can be made via various methods such as credit/debit cards, online banking, or other digital payment options available in the mobile app. - Payment Confirmation: Upon successful payment, users will receive a confirmation receipt and booking details via email and within the app.

3.Balance Payment:

- Due Date: The remaining balance must be paid before the day of the photoshoot. Users will receive reminders via app notifications as the due date approaches. - Payment Methods: Similar to the advance payment, the balance can be paid through the same digital payment options available in the app. - Final Confirmation: Once the balance payment is received, users will receive a final confirmation and detailed itinerary for the photoshoot day.

4.Cancellation and Refund Policy:

-No Cancellations: Booked photoshoots cannot be cancelled by the user. This policy is in place to ensure that our photographers and resources are efficiently managed. - No Refunds: Payments made (both the advance deposit and the balance) are non-refundable. Users are advised to be certain about their booking details before confirming the payment.

5. Rescheduling:

- Rescheduling Requests: Users may request to reschedule their photoshoot up to 3 days before the scheduled date. Rescheduling is subject to availability and must be done through the mobile app or by contacting customer support. - Rescheduling Fee: A nominal fee may apply for rescheduling to cover administrative costs. Users will be informed of any applicable fees at the time of the rescheduling request.

6.Failure to Pay Balance:

- Payment Reminder: Users will receive reminders to pay the remaining balance before the photoshoot date. - Booking Forfeiture: If the balance is not paid by the due date, the booking may be forfeited, and the advance payment will not be refunded. Users are strongly encouraged to complete their payments on time to avoid any inconvenience.

7. Exceptional Circumstances:

- Force Majeure: In the event of unforeseen circumstances such as natural disasters, severe weather conditions, or other force majeure events, Fiche reserves the right to reschedule the photoshoot without additional charges. Users will be notified as soon as possible in such cases. - Health and Safety: If a user or photographer falls ill or faces a health emergency, the photoshoot can be rescheduled without a fee. Documentation may be required for verification.

8. Communication and Support:

- Customer Support: For any booking or payment-related inquiries, users can contact our customer support team through the app, email, or phone. Our support team is available to assist with any issues or questions. - Notifications: Users will receive all important notifications related to their booking, payments, and reminders through email and app notifications to ensure they are always informed. By adhering to this comprehensive Booking and Payment Policy, Fiche aims to provide a smooth and reliable experience for users while ensuring the efficient management of our services and resources.
